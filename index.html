About

I’m a UX Designer with about one year of experience in designing user-centered digital interfaces. I mainly use Figma to bring ideas to life and create intuitive experiences. Alongside design, I’m an Artificial Intelligence student at Benha University, passionate about learning how intelligent systems can enhance digital products. I’ve also had freelancing experience in UX, which gave me the chance to work with clients and understand their needs. I’m always curious, motivated to learn, and eager to grow in both the UX and AI fields.

What I do

I design user-friendly digital experiences with a focus on clarity and usability. I create wireframes, prototypes, and interfaces that solve real problems and make products easy to use. I also enjoy freelancing projects where I turn client needs into practical design solutions.

Interests

I’m passionate about UX/UI design, Artificial Intelligence, and how technology can improve people’s everyday lives. I’m also interested in continuous learning, exploring new design trends, and combining AI with design to build smarter products.
